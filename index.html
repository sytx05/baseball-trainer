About the rules: responsibilities follow SBMSA's 2025 baseball rulebook. SBMSA groups 7U & 8U together as the coach-pitch Midget League (identical rules), and 9U as the kid-pitch Pee Wee League. Within those bounds, cutoff/relay and backup details still vary by coaching staff and game situation. Every responsibility lives in the computeAssignments() function near the bottom of this file — open it in any text editor to fine-tune it. Nothing is sent anywhere; the app runs entirely on your computer.
